AI Advice



We know that it get really risky to diagnose a cancer tumor type wrongly cause it will risk the life of a human being,
especially if the diagnose was a false negative case in breast cancer which have the moast second death rate all over the world.

Here comes BCCP to provide a free to use AI advice to help doctors and specialists determain the tumor type of breast cancer mass with about 97% accuracy and a 0.006% false negative rate.

Instructions: In oreder to use our AI advice you need to prepare some data about the nucleus mass of a FNA (fine needle aspiration) of a breast mass.
it requeires the following:

  • nucleus radius (mean of distances from center to points on the perimeter).
  • texture (standard deviation of gray-scale values). can be obtained from the FNA images
  • nucleus perimeter.
  • nucleus area.
  • nucleus smoothness (local variation in radius lengths).
  • nucleus compactness (perimeter^2 / area - 1.0).
  • nucleus concavity (severity of concave portions of the contour).
  • nucleus concave points (number of concave portions of the contour).
  • nucleus symmetry degree.
  • nucleus fractal dimension ("coastline approximation" - 1).
